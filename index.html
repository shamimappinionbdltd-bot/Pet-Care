Privacy Policy for Pet Care

Developer: BrightLeaf
Last Updated: February 21, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is our absolute priority. This Pet Care App is designed as a "local-first" tool. This means we do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, health records, or financial information from our users with any external servers or third parties.

2. Data Storage & Technology

To ensure maximum privacy, all data you input—including pet profiles, medication schedules, and health logs—is stored locally on your device using internal database technology (SQLite). We have no access to this data, and it is never synchronized to a cloud service.

3. Device Permissions

To provide essential pet care reminders, the app utilizes the following device features:

  • Local Notifications: We request notification access via the permission_handler package. This is used solely to trigger alerts for your pet’s tasks (e.g., feeding times, vet appointments). These alerts are generated entirely on your device.

The app does not use or request access to your camera, location, contacts, or microphone.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs. Your app usage remains completely anonymous to us and any third-party entities.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Since all data exists only on your device, you have complete sovereignty over it. To permanently erase all information associated with the app, you can simply uninstall the application or clear the app's data in your device settings.
